Trollveggen

Experience the valley with the emerald green river Rauma, the steep mountainsides, beautiful bridges and lush waterfalls.

Romsdalen is long and narrow with high majestic mountains on each side. On the valley floor, the traditional salmon river Rauma foams in wild and beautiful nature. At Verma, Rauma goes through a 30-40 meter high gorge and forms the Slettafossen waterfall. The waterfall is secured with fences on each side and has a bridge directly across. The Troll Wall is Europe's tallest vertical and overhanging mountain wall at a total of 1000 meters.

It was in the Romsdal Alps the cradle of climbing sports stood in Europe around 1880, and here are still several of the world's most difficult climbing routes. On the opposite side of the Troll wall, the Romsdalshorn rises to a height of 1555 meters. The Romsdalshorn is a favorite climbing destination for both national and international climbers.
