Geirangerfjorden

The jewel in the fjord crown

Geirangerfjord is the jewel in the crown of the Norwegian fjords. It is a fairytale landscape with its majestic, snow-covered mountain tops, wild and beautiful waterfalls, lush green vegetation and the deep, blue fjord. Take a fjord sightseeing trip and experience it all. Or take the roads that twist and turn through one hairpin bend after another to Geiranger Skywalk- Mt. Dalsnibba, Flydalsjuvet and Ørnesvingen, to experience a fantastic, panoramic view of one of most beautiful tourist destinations in the world, which has been included on UNESCO`s list of World Heritage sites since 2005 and is considered to be among the most scenically outstanding fjord area on the planet.
