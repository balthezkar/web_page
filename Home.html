Explore Romsdalen

Møre and Romsdal is known for its high mountain peaks, green fjords, beautiful roads and refreshing scenery. Some of the attractions are world famous and get more than 300,000 tourists from around the world every year.

We have selected our 4 favorites that we think everyone should visit. Here we cover the entire "must see" list of spectacular mountains, viewpoints, green fjords and gorgeous car trips. Møre and Romsdal is definitely a county worth visiting!
